What we do

Topaz makes software that learns in real time. A Topaz program is like an agent, but instead of an outside agent guessing, rewriting, and re-testing it, the program improves itself as it runs, from real signal.

It is deep technology underneath and plain to use on top: you point it at a goal and live feedback, and it gets better on its own.

Why it matters

Adaptive AI is not new. Models that learn and adapt have run in production for years. What is new is the cost: standing one up used to take a specialist team and months.

Topaz collapses that to an agent building it in minutes. The depth is real; you do not have to be a researcher to use it.

The first product

A landing page that optimizes itself, learning in real time from every visitor, instead of waiting for A/B tests a small site can never run.

Underneath, it runs on Sutra, our language for programs that are continuous and learnable.

What we're building

The product sits on a deeper stack. Each layer is a real, working artifact, not a roadmap promise.

  • Sutra, our language. Logical operations over vector spaces compile into differentiable tensor graphs, so whole programs are continuous and learnable. Published on PyPI as sutra-dev (v0.7.0), with a NeurIPS 2026 submission.
  • Topaz OS, a GPU-native neuro-symbolic operating system written in Sutra, where the whole running system is one inspectable network. Working prototype with a terminal, calculator, and click-driven GUI.
  • Self-optimizing pages, the first product: the stack applied at ground level, where the page itself learns.
