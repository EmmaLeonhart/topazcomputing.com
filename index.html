Vector symbolic architecture for analog hardware and edge AI.

What we do

Topaz makes advanced AI run directly on small, low-power devices (phones, sensors, and hardware out in the world) instead of sending everything back to a distant data center.

We do it with a different way of computing: information becomes patterns of numbers, and the reasoning over it comes down to the same simple piece of arithmetic, repeated. That keeps the work light enough to happen right where the data is.

Why it matters

That one simple operation is exactly what a new generation of ultra-efficient chips, including analog hardware, is built to do: very fast, at very low power.

So work that normally needs the cloud can happen on the device itself, in real time, keeping your data private, without a constant internet connection.
